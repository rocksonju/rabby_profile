■ DSE UPDATE
 
DSEX closed Wednesday's session (08 Jul, the last completed session — today's market has not yet opened at compile time) at 5,770, returning -0.19% for the day (-11 points), as profit-taking in blue-chip stocks pulled the benchmark lower for a second straight session; selective buying in momentum-driven small and mid-caps limited the decline. Turnover fell 16.7% to BDT 1,160cr from BDT 1,380cr the previous session. Breadth was negative: of 396 issues traded, 145 advanced, 187 declined and 64 were unchanged. Textiles led turnover (21.0%), followed by general insurance (9.6%) and engineering (8.8%); travel (+2.8%), IT (+2.1%) and textiles (+1.4%) were the day's best-performing sectors, while financial institutions (-1.3%), cement (-1.1%) and banking (-0.9%) lagged. CSE also ended lower (CSCX -33.3pts, CASPI -47.2pts). YTD the DSEX has returned +18.59% (base: 4,865.34 on 30 Dec 2025).
